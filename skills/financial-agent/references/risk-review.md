# Risk review

Assume the thesis is wrong and look for the mechanism.

- What must already be priced in for the base case to work?
- Which revenue, margin, reinvestment, financing, customer, supplier, regulatory, governance, and currency assumptions can fail?
- Which portfolio positions or themes make this exposure correlated rather than diversifying?
- What evidence is independent of management?
- Which reported metric can improve while economic quality deteriorates?
- What observable condition invalidates the thesis, and over what period?
- What remains unknown because the source set cannot answer it?

Rank risks by thesis damage and observability. Do not turn uncertainty into fake numerical probabilities.
